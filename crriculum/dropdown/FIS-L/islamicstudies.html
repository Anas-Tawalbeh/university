Course format

  • Type

    • Undergraduate 4 year program

  • Language used

    • Thai and foreign languages (Arabic, English, Malay)

  • Admission

    • Accepting students to study in the program, both Thai and foreign students who can use the Thai language.

Course Operation

  • Date - time of instruction

    • Semester 1: June - October
    • Semester 2: November - March
    • Summer semester: April - May

  • Qualifications of students

    • Entrants must meet the requirements of Fatoni University regulations. Regarding undergraduate education, 2019, as follows:
    • Eligibility according to Fatoni University Regulations Regarding undergraduate education, 2019
    • 1) Be a graduate of high school (Grade 6) or equivalent
    • 2) Not being a person with disgraceful behavior or being expelled from any higher education institution due to disciplinary offenses.
    • 3) Not having a serious communicable disease or a disease that is an obstacle to study.
    • 4) Be selected as a student through the selection process set by the university.
