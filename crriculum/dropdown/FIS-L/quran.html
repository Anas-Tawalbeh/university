Course Operation

  • Date - time of instruction

    • Semester 1: June - October
    • Semester 2: November - March
    • Summer semester: April - May

  • Qualifications of students

    • Entrants must meet the requirements of Fatoni University regulations. Regarding undergraduate education, 2014, as follows:
    • Eligibility according to Fatoni University Regulations Regarding undergraduate education, 2019
    • 1) Be a graduate of high school (M. A. 5 or M. 6 ) or equivalent
    • 2) Not being a person with disgraceful behavior or being expelled from any higher education institution. due to disciplinary offense
    • 3) Not having a serious communicable disease or a disease that is an obstacle to study.
    • 4) Be selected as a student through the selection process set by the university.
    • 5) Must pass the Arabic Language Proficiency Test. by determining the score level as follows:
      • 5.1) A score of 80 points or more will be able to study in the first year of the program without having to pass the pre-Arab language course at the International Language Institute. Fatoni University
      • 5.2) Scores less than 80 points are required to study pre-Arabic language at the International Language Institute. Fatoni University according to the specified criteria Before entering the first year of the program for a period of one year.
