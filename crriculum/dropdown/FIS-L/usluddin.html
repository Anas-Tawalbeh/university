Course format

  • Type

    • Undergraduate 4 year program

  • Language used

    • Curriculum in Education as a Foreign Language (Arabic)

  • Admission

    • Accepting students to study in the program, both Thai and foreign students

Course Operation

  • Date - Time to conduct the course

    • Semester 1: June-September
    • Semester 2: October-February
    • Summer semester, March-May

  • Qualifications of students

    • Be a high school graduate (M. A. 5 or M. 6) or equivalent
    • Eligibility according to Fatoni University Regulations Regarding undergraduate education, 2019
    • Be selected as students through the selection process set by the university.
    • Pass the Arabic language proficiency test as specified by the course.
    • Passed the Arabic Language Proficiency Test by determining the score level as follows:
    • Have a score of 80 points or more to be able to study in the first year of the program without having to pass the language preparatory course at the International Language institute Fatoni University.
    • Have a score of less than 80 points, required to study language preparation at the International Language Institute according to the specified criteria. Before entering the 1st year of the program for a period of 1 or 2 semesters
