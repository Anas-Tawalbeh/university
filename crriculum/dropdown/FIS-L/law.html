Course format

  • Type

    • Undergraduate 4 year program

  • Language used

    • Thai language

  • Admission

    • Be a graduate of at least high school or its equivalent and have qualifications according to Fatoni University's Regulations on Undergraduate Studies, 2014

  • Cooperation with other institutions

    • Do not Have

  • Awarding degrees to graduates

    • Give a degree in only one field of study and in the case of bachelor's degree holder in any field or a branch close to this field of study. Able to enroll as a second degree by being exempted from having to register for courses in the general education category and able to transfer courses in other categories more or less different according to the field of study that has been completed.

Course Operation

  • Date - time of instruction

    • Teaching and learning are held from Sunday to Thursday and may be taught on other days according to reasons and necessity that the Program Committee deems appropriate.
    • First semester: June - September
    • Last semester: November - February
    • Summer semester: March - April

  • Qualifications of students

    • Be a graduate of at least high school or its equivalent and have qualifications according to Fatoni University's regulations on Undergraduate Studies, 2014

Occupations that can be engaged after graduation

  • Lawyer/Legal Adviser
  • Academics/personnel in government and private sectors
  • Legal training course
  • Engage in a freelance career in law, such as a lawyer (through training from the Lawyer Council's Office of Vocabulary Training)
  • Government service such as police officer, Phra Thammanoon judicial officers, District Clerk, Dato Justice, etc.
